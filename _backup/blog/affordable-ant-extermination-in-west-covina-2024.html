Common Ant Species in West Covina and How to Spot Them

West Covina's diverse neighborhoods, including Merced and Northview, host various ant types. Argentine ants form large supercolonies, creating visible trails along sidewalks and countertops. Carpenter ants, common in wooded areas like Ramona, bore into wood, potentially weakening structures.

  • Look for foraging lines near windows, doors, or sinks
  • Check for sawdust-like frass indicating carpenter ants
  • Monitor sugary spills or pet food bowls for attraction

Early detection is key. If you notice these signs in your West End property, contact us immediately for a free inspection.
